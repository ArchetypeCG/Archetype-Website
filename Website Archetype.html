About

Clarity first. Development second. Impact always.

Archetype Coaching Group works with individuals, teams and businesses who want development to feel purposeful, premium and connected to real outcomes. We help clients understand what is happening now, what needs to improve and what support will create the strongest impact.

The work is built around listening, diagnosis and practical action. This means clients are not pushed into a standard programme. Instead, we begin by understanding the person, team or business need, then design the right route forward.

Our client promise

We help you make confident decisions before you invest in development. The aim is always to understand the need first, then recommend the right support — not simply sell a programme.

Development that feels considered, not generic.

Archetype Coaching Group helps people and organisations make confident decisions before investing in training, coaching or consultancy.

For businesses

We support employers with training reviews, skills audits, diagnostic assessments, management development, apprenticeship support and consultancy that improves performance and confidence.

For individuals

We help individuals reflect, reset and move forward through coaching, confidence-building, career direction, skills development and structured personal action planning.

Questions

Before you get in touch.

A few quick answers to help you decide whether Archetype Coaching Group is the right fit.

Is the initial consultation free?

Yes. The first consultation is free and is used to understand your goals, challenges and whether our support is the right fit.

Can you review our existing training?

Yes. We can complete a training review to explore current provision, identify gaps and recommend practical improvements.

Do you offer skills audits and diagnostic assessments?

Yes. We use skills audits and diagnostic assessments to understand strengths, development needs and the most appropriate support route.

Can support be tailored to our organisation?

Yes. Support can be shaped around your aims, staff, learners, timescales and current challenges.
